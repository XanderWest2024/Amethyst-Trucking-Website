Our Story

More Than Just A Paintjob.

Established in 2026, Amethyst Trucking was founded on the principles of respect, realism, and camaraderie. We aren't just driving trucks in a simulator; we are building a community of enthusiasts who love the open road.

Whether you haul heavy cargo in ATS or cruise the autobahns in ETS2, there is a spot in our convoy for you. We host weekly public convoys on TruckersMP and maintain a standard of excellence.
